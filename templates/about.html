About Scanalyze

Welcome to Scanalyze, an advanced AI-powered image analysis and forensics tool built for accuracy and insight. 🚀

Scanalyze empowers you to perform background removal, edge detection, histogram analysis, AI-based classification and image metadata extraction with ease and precision.

Whether you're a developer, designer, researcher, or forensics analyst, this tool brings AI and image intelligence right to your fingertips.

✨ Features at a Glance

  • 🔹 AI-Based Image Classification (VGG16 & VGG19)
  • 🔹 Background Removal
  • 🔹 Edge Detection (Horizontal & Vertical)
  • 🔹 Metadata Extraction (EXIF info)
  • 🔹 Image Similarity Comparison

🛡️ Why Use Scanalyze?

Scanalyze is perfect for anyone who wants fast, intelligent, and reliable image insights. From forensic professionals analyzing tampered content to creators looking for metadata and structure, Scanalyze helps you decode what the eye can’t see.

🔍 Role in Digital Forensics

Images can hide stories—Scanalyze helps uncover them. It’s built to assist with:

  • 🔸 Verifying authenticity of images
  • 🔸 Extracting metadata like timestamps, location & camera info
  • 🔸 Detecting tampering through pixel comparison
  • 🔸 Matching images by visual similarity and AI classification
